Jellyfish are invertebrates that can help with transporting nutrients throughout the ocean.

There are many types of Jellyfish, such as:

  • Box Jellyfish
  • Immortal Jellyfish
  • Moon Jellyfish

    • Jellyfish can be very deadly, and some aren't dangerous at all! Below is a table of which jellyfish are deadly or not.

    Dangerous Not dangerous
    Box Jellyfish Moon Jellyfish
    Flower Hat Jellyfish Crystal Jellyfish
    Sea Nettle Jellyfish Cannonball Jellyfish
    Irukandji Jellyfish Upside-Down Jellyfish
